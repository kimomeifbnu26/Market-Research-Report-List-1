<p><p>The Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) accelerometer market is categorized into two main types: mono-axial and multi-axial accelerometers. Mono-axial FBG accelerometers measure acceleration along a single axis, making them suitable for applications with directional constraints. In contrast, multi-axial FBG accelerometers can detect acceleration in multiple directions simultaneously, allowing for more comprehensive monitoring in complex environments. These advanced sensing technologies are increasingly utilized in fields such as structural health monitoring, aerospace, and automotive industries for enhanced performance and reliability.</p></p>
