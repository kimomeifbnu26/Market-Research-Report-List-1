<p><strong>The Cast Acrylic Sheets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Continuous Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cast acrylic sheets market is primarily divided into two types: cell cast acrylic sheets and continuous cast acrylic sheets. Cell cast acrylic sheets are produced by pouring molten acrylic between two glass sheets, resulting in high clarity and optical quality, often used in applications requiring precision and visual appeal. Continuous cast acrylic sheets, on the other hand, are manufactured through a continuous process that allows for larger, uniform sheets, making them ideal for bulk applications and cost-effectiveness. Both types serve diverse industries, including signage, displays, and protective barriers.</p></p>
