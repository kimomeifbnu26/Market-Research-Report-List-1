<p><p>The wind speed alarm market is divided into two primary types: digital and analog wind speed alarms. Digital wind speed alarms utilize electronic sensors and microcontrollers to provide precise readings and advanced features like programmable alerts and data logging. In contrast, analog wind speed alarms display wind speed through mechanical means, often using a dial and pointer, which makes them more straightforward but less feature-rich. Both types serve vital safety and operational purposes in various industries, including meteorology and aviation.</p></p>
