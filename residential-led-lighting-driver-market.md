<p><p>The Residential LED Lighting Driver market encompasses various types of dimming and control technologies. DALI (Digital Addressable Lighting Interface) allows for advanced, programmable dimming and control. 0-10V Dimming enables seamless dimming by varying voltage levels. Standard (non-dim) drivers provide fixed output without dimming capabilities. Triac Dimming uses phase-cut technology, commonly found in traditional systems. Smart Drivers integrate with smart home systems for remote control and automation, offering enhanced functionality and energy management capabilities for residential settings.</p></p>
