<p><ul><li>"Bonding Batter"</li><li>"Coating Batter"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fried batter markets can be categorized primarily into two types: bonding batter and coating batter. Bonding batter is used to adhere ingredients together, enhancing the structural integrity of items like fritters or tempura. It creates a cohesive layer that ensures ingredients remain intact during frying. Coating batter, on the other hand, provides a crispy outer layer to various foods, enhancing their flavor and texture. Both types are essential in the food industry, offering unique textures to fried products.</p></p>
