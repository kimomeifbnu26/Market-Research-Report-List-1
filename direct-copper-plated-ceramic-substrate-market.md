<p><p>The Direct Copper-Plated Ceramic Substrate market primarily features Al₂O₃ (alumina) and AlN (aluminum nitride) ceramic substrates. Al₂O₃ substrates are known for their excellent electrical insulation, high thermal conductivity, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various electronic applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics, enhancing device efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
