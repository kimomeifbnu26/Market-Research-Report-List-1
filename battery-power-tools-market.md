<p><strong>The Battery Power Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brushed Motor</li><li>Brushless Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The battery power tools market features two main types of motors: brushed and brushless. Brushed motors use carbon brushes to transfer electricity, which can result in more wear and lower efficiency but are generally less expensive. Brushless motors eliminate brushes, offering greater efficiency, longer run times, and reduced maintenance. While brushed motors are commonly found in budget-friendly tools, brushless motors are increasingly preferred for professional applications due to their performance and durability advantages.</p></p>
