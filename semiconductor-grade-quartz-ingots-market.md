<p><p>The semiconductor-grade quartz ingots market consists of several types, each catering to specific applications. Electrically fused quartz ingots are produced through melting high-purity quartz in electric furnaces, offering excellent thermal and chemical resistance. Flame fused quartz ingots are created by melting quartz using a flame, providing high purity and low contamination. Synthetic fused quartz ingots are manufactured through controlled processes, ensuring uniformity and quality. Opaque quartz ingots, on the other hand, contain impurities, making them suitable for specialized applications where transparency is not critical.</p></p>
