<p><p>The 3D induction system market features three key types: Binocular Stereo Vision, which employs two cameras to mimic human depth perception for object recognition and distance measurement; 3D Structured Light, which projects patterns onto surfaces to capture intricate shapes and textures; and Time-of-Flight (TOF) technology, which measures distances by calculating the time taken for light to reflect off objects. Each technology offers unique advantages in applications like robotics, automation, and augmented reality, enhancing spatial awareness and accuracy.</p></p>
