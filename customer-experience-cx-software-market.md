<p><p>Customer Experience (CX) software can be categorized into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based solutions. On-Premise software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and customization, but often requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based software is hosted remotely, allowing for easy access, scalability, and lower initial costs, as it typically operates on a subscription model. Both types aim to enhance customer interactions and improve overall satisfaction.</p></p>
