<p><p>The Frequency Domain Terahertz Spectrometer market is divided into single-channel and dual-channel types. Single-channel spectrometers utilize one detector to analyze terahertz radiation, resulting in straightforward, cost-effective solutions suitable for basic applications. In contrast, dual-channel spectrometers employ two detectors, enabling simultaneous measurement of multiple wavelengths, which enhances data acquisition speed and resolution. This dual-channel configuration is ideal for complex analyses, making it beneficial for advanced research and industrial applications, where precision and efficiency are paramount.</p></p>
