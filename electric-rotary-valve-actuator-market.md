<p><p>The Electric Rotary Valve Actuator Market is primarily divided into two types: Directly Attached Type and Pedestal Type. Directly Attached Type actuators are mounted directly on the valve, offering compactness and simplicity for easy installation and efficient performance. In contrast, Pedestal Type actuators are mounted on a separate pedestal, allowing for greater accessibility and ease of maintenance, especially in larger installations. Both types enhance automation in industrial processes by enabling precise control of fluid flow and pressure.</p></p>
