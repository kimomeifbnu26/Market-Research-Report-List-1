<p><ul><li>Inductive Transformer</li><li>Capacitive Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Innovative Discrete Network Transformers market encompasses two primary types: inductive transformers and capacitive transformers. Inductive transformers utilize electromagnetic induction to transfer energy through coils, making them suitable for voltage regulation and power conversion. In contrast, capacitive transformers rely on electric fields between conductors to facilitate energy transfer, often used in applications requiring high-frequency signals. Both types play crucial roles in modern electrical networks, enhancing efficiency, reliability, and performance in various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
