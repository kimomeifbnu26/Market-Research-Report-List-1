<p><p>Crane limit switches are essential safety devices that control the movement of cranes by signaling when to stop or change direction. The inching switch allows for precise, slow movement of the crane, enabling operators to make fine adjustments during load positioning. On the other hand, speed switches monitor the crane's operational speed, ensuring it remains within safe limits. Both types are critical for enhancing safety, improving operational efficiency, and preventing accidents in heavy lifting environments.</p></p>
