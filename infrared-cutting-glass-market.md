<p><strong>The Infrared Cutting Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Type</li><li>Reflection Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared cutting glass market is segmented into absorption type and reflection type. Absorption type infrared cutting glass absorbs specific wavelengths of infrared radiation, converting it into heat, which aids in the cutting process. This type is ideal for materials that require precise thermal control. On the other hand, reflection type infrared cutting glass reflects infrared wavelengths, minimizing heat absorption and ensuring a cooler working environment. Both types play crucial roles in various industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and precision in cutting operations.</p></p>
