<p><p>The rubber suspension bushes market is categorized into two primary types: natural rubber suspension bushes and synthetic rubber suspension bushes. Natural rubber bushes are derived from latex and offer excellent elasticity and resilience, making them suitable for various automotive applications. In contrast, synthetic rubber bushes, made from man-made polymers, provide enhanced durability, resistance to temperature variations, and superior performance in harsher environments. Each type serves distinct needs in vehicle dynamics, contributing to comfort and stability while absorbing shocks and vibrations.</p></p>
