<p><p>The Simply Breathing Apparatus market consists of two main types: open-circuit and closed-circuit systems. Open-circuit systems allow exhaled air to escape into the environment, making them suitable for short-duration activities like swimming. Closed-circuit systems recycle the exhaled air, removing carbon dioxide and adding fresh oxygen, making them ideal for longer, controlled environments like deep-sea diving or space missions. Each type serves distinct user needs based on duration, environment, and safety considerations in breathing applications.</p></p>
