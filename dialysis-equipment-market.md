<p><strong>The Dialysis Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis Equipment</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysis Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dialysis equipment market comprises two primary types: hemodialysis equipment and peritoneal dialysis equipment. Hemodialysis equipment includes machines that filter blood externally using a dialyzer, typically used in clinical settings for patients with kidney failure. In contrast, peritoneal dialysis equipment facilitates the exchange of dialysis solution inside the abdominal cavity, allowing patients to perform the procedure at home. Both segments cater to the growing demand for efficient kidney treatment solutions, driven by increasing incidences of chronic kidney disease.</p></p>
