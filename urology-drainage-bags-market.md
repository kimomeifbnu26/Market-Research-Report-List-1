<p><p>The urology drainage bags market includes two primary types: leg bags and night drainage bags. Leg bags are compact and designed for daytime use, allowing patients to maintain mobility and comfort while collecting urine. They are typically strapped to the leg and can be discreetly worn under clothing. Night drainage bags, on the other hand, are larger and used during sleep, accommodating higher volumes of urine. They feature a longer tubing system, allowing for uninterrupted collection overnight and minimizing the risk of leakage.</p></p>
