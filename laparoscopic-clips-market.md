<p><ul><li>Polymeric Absorbable Clips</li><li>Metallic Clips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laparoscopic clips market is divided into two primary types: polymeric absorbable clips and metallic clips. Polymeric absorbable clips are designed to dissolve in the body over time, reducing the need for additional surgeries and minimizing foreign body reactions. In contrast, metallic clips, often made of titanium or stainless steel, offer permanent fixation and are used for secure tissue approximation. Both clip types are essential in minimally invasive surgical procedures, enhancing patient recovery and surgical outcomes.</p></p>
