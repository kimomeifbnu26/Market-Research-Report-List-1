<p><ul><li>Human Advisory</li><li>Robo Advisory</li><li>Hybrid Advisory</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wealth management encompasses three primary market types: human advisory, robo advisory, and hybrid advisory. Human advisory relies on personalized guidance from financial advisors who assess individual client needs and provide tailored investment strategies. Robo advisory utilizes automated algorithms to create and manage portfolios, offering a cost-effective and efficient alternative with limited personal interaction. Hybrid advisory combines the strengths of both approaches, providing clients with direct access to human advisors while utilizing technology for efficient portfolio management and low fees.</p></p>
