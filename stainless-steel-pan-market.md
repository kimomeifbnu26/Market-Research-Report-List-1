<p><p>The stainless steel pan market is categorized into three main types: 200 Series, 300 Series, and 400 Series stainless steel. The 200 Series contains manganese and is cost-effective, offering decent corrosion resistance and durability. The 300 Series, particularly 304, is the most widely used in cookware, known for its superior corrosion resistance and non-reactive properties, making it ideal for food applications. The 400 Series is more affordable but has lower corrosion resistance, often used for specific cooking tasks like searing and frying.</p></p>
