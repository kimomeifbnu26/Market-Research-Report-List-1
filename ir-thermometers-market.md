<p><ul><li>Ear</li><li>Forehead</li><li>Multifunction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IR thermometer market consists of three primary types: ear, forehead, and multifunction thermometers. Ear thermometers use infrared technology to measure temperature inside the ear, providing quick results and are often preferred for children. Forehead thermometers measure surface temperature from the skin, making them non-invasive and easy to use. Multifunction thermometers combine features to measure temperature in various ways, including oral, rectal, or ear methods, offering versatility for different user preferences and situations.</p></p>
