<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Amblyopia Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Deprivation Amblyopia</li><li>Refractive Amblyopia</li><li>Strabismic Amblyopia</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The amblyopia treatment market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Deprivation Amblyopia** occurs when visual development is hindered by an obstructed pathway, often due to cataracts, necessitating surgical intervention or corrective lenses. </p><p>2. **Refractive Amblyopia** arises from significant differences in refractive error between the eyes, typically treated with corrective eyewear and vision therapy. </p><p>3. **Strabismic Amblyopia** is linked to misalignment of the eyes, often requiring treatments like patching, vision therapy, or surgery to improve coordination and vision.</p></p>
