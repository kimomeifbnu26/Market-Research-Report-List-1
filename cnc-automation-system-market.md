<p><p>The CNC automation system market is segmented into turning, milling, and grinding categories. Turning involves rotating a workpiece against a cutting tool for precision shaping, commonly used in producing cylindrical parts. Milling employs rotating cutters to remove material, allowing for complex shapes and features on various surfaces. Grinding focuses on achieving fine finishes and precise dimensions by using abrasives, ideal for hard materials. Each type enhances efficiency, accuracy, and productivity in industrial manufacturing applications.</p></p>
