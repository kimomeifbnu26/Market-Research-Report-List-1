<p><p>The Carbon Blacks for Non-rubber Applications market primarily consists of two main types: Thermal Black and Furnace Black. Thermal Black is produced through the thermal decomposition of hydrocarbons and is used in applications like plastics and coatings for its excellent conductivity and reinforcement properties. Furnace Black, on the other hand, is generated through the combustion of hydrocarbons in a furnace and is widely utilized in inks, paints, and specialty polymers due to its consistent particle size and high coloration properties.</p></p>
