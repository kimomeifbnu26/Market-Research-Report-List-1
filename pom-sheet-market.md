<p><ul><li>Homo-polyoxymethylene</li><li>Co-polyoxymethylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The POM sheet market is categorized into two main types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene is a single-type polymer known for its excellent mechanical properties and high chemical resistance, making it suitable for strict engineering applications. In contrast, co-polyoxymethylene consists of two different monomers, providing enhanced properties like better impact resistance and flexibility. Both types are widely used in various industries, including automotive, electronics, and consumer goods, due to their durability and performance characteristics.</p></p>
