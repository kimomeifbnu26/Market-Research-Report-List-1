<p><p>Automatic Liquid Samplers (ALS) are devices used to automatically collect liquid samples from various processes for analysis. The market is divided into two main types: full-automatic and semi-automatic systems. Full-automatic samplers operate independently, managing the entire sampling process without human intervention, ideal for continuous operations. Semi-automatic systems require some manual input, allowing operators to control specific functions while automating other aspects, making them suitable for applications where flexibility and operator oversight are necessary.</p></p>
