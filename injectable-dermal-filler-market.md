<p><p>The injectable dermal filler market comprises several types, each with unique properties and applications. Hyaluronic Acid (HA) fillers are popular for their ability to retain moisture and provide a natural look, ideal for facial volume restoration. Collagen fillers support skin structure but are less commonly used due to potential allergenic reactions. Calcium Hydroxylapatite (CaHA) fillers stimulate collagen production and offer longer-lasting results. Other fillers include poly-L-lactic acid and polymethylmethacrylate, catering to diverse aesthetic needs and preferences.</p></p>
