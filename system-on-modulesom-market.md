<p><p>A System on Module (SoM) is a compact embedded computing solution that integrates essential components such as the processor, memory, and interfaces onto a single module. SoMs streamline the development process for various embedded applications by providing a pre-validated hardware platform that can be easily integrated into larger systems. This approach reduces design complexity and accelerates time-to-market for products, making SoMs increasingly popular in sectors like IoT, industrial automation, and consumer electronics.</p><p>The System on Module (SoM) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.10% during the forecast period.
