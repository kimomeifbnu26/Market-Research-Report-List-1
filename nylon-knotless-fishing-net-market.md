<p><p>The Nylon Knotless Fishing Net market can be categorized into two main types based on orientation: Depthway and Lengthway. Depthway nets are designed for vertical use, effectively capturing fish in various water depths, making them ideal for deep-sea fishing. In contrast, Lengthway nets are oriented horizontally, suitable for surface or coastal fishing, focusing on species that swim near the water's surface. Both types leverage knotless nylon for reduced snagging and increased durability, catering to different fishing techniques and environments.</p></p>
