<p><strong>The Coated Art Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coated One Side Paper (C1S)</li><li>Coated Two Side Paper (C2S)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coated art paper market features two primary types: Coated One Side (C1S) and Coated Two Side (C2S). C1S paper is coated on one side, offering a glossy finish ideal for high-quality printing applications like posters and promotional materials, while the uncoated side is often used for writing or printing text. In contrast, C2S paper is coated on both sides, providing a uniform finish that enhances color vibrancy, making it suitable for magazines, catalogs, and brochures. Both types are valued for their visual appeal and print performance.</p></p>
